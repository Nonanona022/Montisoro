Which organisations

Sector-agnostic.
From SME to corporate.

Montisoro works for organisations where absence, workability and human complexity are daily reality. What differs is the context — not the system.

Profile 01

Large organisations

Corporates and multinationals with many cases, multiple services and a need for governance, roles and scalable technology.

Profile 02

SME & mid-market

Organisations without their own absence direction, wanting structure and expertise without building a full internal department.

Profile 03

High-absence sectors

Environments with physically or mentally demanding roles, shift work or high pressure, where absence structurally recurs.

Common trait: the organisation already does something about absence, but lacks the overview, the neutral direction or the structure to truly get a grip. It is not effort that is missing — it is system.

Trusted by: among others Volvo Group, Alcon, Novartis, AXA Belgium, Securitas and Vaillant Group — an illustration of the scale and sector breadth in which the approach works.

When it fits
Recognisable?

Three times yes is a pattern.

Montisoro fits when absence can no longer be solved with isolated efforts. These signals point to a system problem, not an effort problem.

  • Cases only become visible once already complex.
  • Signals are scattered across mail, spreadsheets and notes.
  • HR loses time to searching and interpreting.
  • Managers are unsure what they may say.
  • Return to work does not follow one approach.
  • GDPR and documentation feel vulnerable.
  • No view of recurring patterns.
  • Knowledge disappears at role changes.

Doesn't it fit? Montisoro does not replace an occupational physician or recognised prevention service, and is not a solution for a single one-off case without structural ambition.
