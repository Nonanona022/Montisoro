Services
The offering

Not separate services.
Four movements of one system.

Montisoro delivers not isolated interventions but one coherent system around absence and sustainable employability. Four service lines carry that system — usable separately, strongest together.

01

Human Reintegration

Neutral direction between employee, HR, manager and occupational medicine — from the first day of absence to sustainable return to work.

02

Compliance & Governance

Well-being law, labour law, GDPR and compliance translated into workable agreements, roles and documentation.

03

Capability Building

Build internal capacity, clear roles and process discipline — so knowledge stays in the organisation.

04

Technology & Agentic AI

Storm (platform) and Casey AI make care scalable and capability steerable.

Important: Montisoro does not replace the occupational physician or external prevention service. It fills the often-missing director role — neutral, structured and with technology underneath.

Service lines 01–02
The human core

Direction where it gets complex.

01

Human Reintegration

What: neutral, non-clinical case managers direct absence and reintegration trajectories and keep all parties connected. For whom: organisations with frequent or long-term absence that lack an independent director. Result: shorter duration, more sustainable return, less escalation.

02

Compliance & Governance

What: legally anchor absence and reintegration policy — well-being law, labour law, GDPR, role allocation and documentation. For whom: organisations wanting to remove compliance risks and vulnerable documentation. Result: clear roles, watertight files, less legal risk.

The role missing in too many organisations: someone who sees everything, directs neutrally and keeps the file watertight.

Service lines 03–04
Making it scalable

Capability that stays.

03

Capability Building

What: make managers and HR stronger in conversations, follow-up and process; anchor roles and discipline. For whom: organisations that do not want to stay dependent on external input. Result: knowledge and capacity stay internal, even after the engagement.

04

Technology & Agentic AI

What: Storm structures cases, roles, communication and governance; Casey AI adds contextual intelligence. For whom: organisations managing absence scattered across mail and spreadsheets. Result: one source of truth, consistent follow-up, scalable care.

Technology without capability creates dependence. Capability with technology creates organisational strength.

Modular: each service line is usable separately. The fit check (see the "Processes" document) helps determine which route — and therefore which service — best serves your organisation today.
