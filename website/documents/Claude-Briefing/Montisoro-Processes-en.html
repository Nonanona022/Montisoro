Process 03
The calculator

Six steps to
a cost picture.

The absence calculator translates six input steps into a substantiated absence report. The same engine feeds screen, PDF and email.

01

Input

Organisation, gross salary, recurring costs, replacement, organisational impact and absence profile.

02

Calculation

The engine computes employer cost (invoiceable) and total cost (incl. organisational impact).

03

Report

Print-ready PDF with benchmark vs the sector, risk level, savings scenarios and priorities.

04

Follow-up

The report opens the conversation; a diagnostic call translates the figure into a concrete approach.

Detail: the full formulas, parameters and sources are in the "Calculator Documentation".

Process 04
Reintegration direction

Neutral direction,
humanly executed.

The case management process keeps all parties connected — employee, HR, manager and occupational medicine — with clear roles and watertight documentation.

1

Intake

Map the situation: case, context, parties and roles.

2

Direction

A neutral case manager steers follow-up, conversations and alignment.

3

Return to work

Structured, sustainable return in coordination with all parties.

4

Anchoring

Insights and patterns fed back into policy and prevention.

Governance underneath: who sees and may do what strictly follows the role within the case. No noise, no oversharing.
