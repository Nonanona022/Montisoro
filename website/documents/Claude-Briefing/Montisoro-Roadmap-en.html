Open actions
Open actions & blockers

What remains before
and after go-live.

A handful of concrete items remain. Most wait on material or decisions — not on development.

K1Production keys & DNS

The Netlify/Resend/Supabase keys and DNS are not yet set. Until then, calculator and form leads do not arrive (mailto fallback active). The final link to launch.

K2Admin access gate

The admin panel has server-side auth + roles. Before the public URL it belongs behind an external access gate (e.g. Cloudflare Access) as a mandatory first layer.

T1Real testimonial quotes

Literal quote + name + role + written consent per client. Until then: honest reference framing (“verified reference on request”).

T2Substantiation of result figures

Measurement period, definition and calculation behind 2,500+ · 30–35% · €14.7M. Ideally as a separate methodology page.

PDFReal booking link

The CTA in the absence report currently points to the contact page; to be replaced by a real calendar link (Calendly / Bookings) once available.

Order: K1 unblocks launch; K2 belongs with it. T1, T2 and the PDF link raise trust further and can run in parallel — they do not block the launch.

Go-live runbook
The steps to launch

No more code.
Just configuration.

An ordered checklist takes the site live. Work top to bottom; skip nothing. Estimated time: one working day plus DNS propagation.

Key variables (K1): SUPABASE_URL · SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY · PDF_BUCKET · RESEND_API_KEY · MAIL_FROM · INTERNAL_NOTIFY_EMAIL · SITE_BASE_URL · ADMIN_BASE_URL · ALLOWED_ORIGIN · ADMIN_PASSWORD · ADMIN_SESSION_SECRET. If one is missing, the functions stay inert (mailto fallback). Rollback is atomic: re-publishing a previous Netlify deploy reverts in seconds.

Pilot, proof and growth.

Once the base is live and stable, the focus shifts from building to proving and scaling — with Casey AI as the engine.

Casey AI · pilot

From September 2026, pilot programmes start with early adopters: early previews, product updates, feature voting and Casey Community Events. They help shape how Casey evolves.

Strengthen the proof

Verified testimonials, a methodology page behind the result figures, and at least one published case PDF. Making what is already strong, stronger.

Security & compliance

A security/DPA page substantiating the Storm claims (GDPR, EU hosting, role-based access, audit logs).

Product depth

Per-sector benchmark in the calculator, an admin write-bridge (notes/lead status back to the DB) and a PDF history view.

The order stays deliberate: live and provable first, then bigger.
